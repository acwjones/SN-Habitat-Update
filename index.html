Habitat Gravity Control Hotfix

Version 1.0 featured a bug in the habitat gravity control systems. This bug is benign in most environments, but could cause catastrophic failure in habitats at risk of flooding. In the event of flooding the bug causes liquids to pool and rise at a constant rate of fill regardless of space or elevation, leading to inferior environmental hazard detection, and drowning.

This update disables artificial gravity in water-rich environments. In the event of flooding, liquids should now behave according to the natural laws of physics. In short, retreating from a hull breach to the uppermost compartment and sealing yourself inside will now effectively delay your demise - but only for a while.
